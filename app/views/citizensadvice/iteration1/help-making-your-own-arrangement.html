Get help making your own child maintenance arrangement

These links will open in a new tab. This page will stay open so you can come back to it at any time.

On GOV.UK

Working out the cost of raising your children
This guide helps you think about the things your child needs and how much they cost, to help you work out a child maintenance amount.

Calculate your child maintenance
You can get an estimate of how much you would pay or receive if you used the Child Maintenance Service. You can use this amount to discuss making your own arrangement with the other parent.

Planning your child maintenance conversation with the other parent
This guide can help you plan a conversation about child maintenance with your child’s other parent.

Recording your agreement with the other parent
You can use this form to record the agreement you make with your child’s other parent. This is not legally binding but signing it shows you have both agreed to the arrangement.

On Citizens Advice

Agreeing maintenance between you Making a private or ‘family-based’ arrangement can suit some parents. There are no specific rules, and you can include whatever you want in the arrangement to help support your child as long as you both agree.

Using mediation to help you separate As a third person who won’t take sides, a mediator can help you sort out differences between you and your ex-partner. This is a step that you can take before you go to a solicitor to try and resolve your situation.

On CAFCASS

Parenting Plan The Parenting Plan is a written plan worked out between parents after they separate which covers the practical issues of parenting, putting the bests interests of the children first. There are resources to create and support a Parenting Plan for your family.

Give your feedback

If you want to make your own child maintenance arrangement, you can fill in this survey to help us improve this service.
